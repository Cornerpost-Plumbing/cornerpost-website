- Sheet headers should be:
  Reference Number | Timestamp | Name | Phone | Email | Street Address | City | State | Service | Preferred Time | Message | Photo Links | Status

Note on reference numbers:
- Apps Script generates the true reference/job number and stores it in the sheet/email.
- Because Google Apps Script does not reliably allow readable cross-origin POST responses from a static website, the customer success message does not display the generated reference number.
- The customer sees a polished confirmation card and your urgent phone number.
